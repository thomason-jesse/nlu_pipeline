\subsection{Detailed Description}
(Sphinx 3.\-0 specific) Gaussian density module. Gaussian density distribution implementation. There are two major difference bettwen ms\-\_\-gauden and cont\-\_\-mgau. One is the fact that ms\-\_\-gauden only take cares of the Gaussian computation part where cont\-\_\-mgau actually take care of senone computation as well. The other is the fact that ms\-\_\-gauden is a multi-\/stream implementation of G\-M\-M computation.
