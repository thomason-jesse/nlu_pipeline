These are personalized steps for the Sphinx acoustic model adaptation process. 
The instructions here are not complete and hence, this should be used
in conjunction with the instructions on the Sphinx wiki. 

Generating acoustic features files step:

sphinx_fe -argfile en-us/feat.params -samprate 16000 -c adaptation.fileids -di . -do . -eo mfc -raw yes

Accumulating observation count step:

NOTE: Look for WARN statmenents in this step to find words not supported by dictionary. These should be added 
	  to dictionary before proceeding. 

./bw -hmmdir en-us -moddeffn en-us/mdef -ts2cbfn .ptm. -feat 1s_c_d_dd -svspec 0-12/13-25/26-38 -cmn current -agc none -dictfn cmudict-en-us.dict -ctlfn adaptation.fileids -lsnfn transcription.txt -accumdir .

MAP adapt step:

./map_adapt -moddeffn en-us/mdef -ts2cbfn .ptm.     -meanfn en-us/means     -varfn en-us/variances     -mixwfn en-us/mixture_weights     -tmatfn en-us/transition_matrices     -accumdir .     -mapmeanfn en-us-adapt/means     -mapvarfn en-us-adapt/variances     -mapmixwfn en-us-adapt/mixture_weights     -maptmatfn en-us-adapt/transition_matrices

Testing step:

NOTE: Make sure audio files are in their own folder and that they have the .raw extension. 

pocketsphinx_batch -adcin yes -cepdir files -cepext .raw -ctl adaptation.fileids -lm fifty_fifty.lm.bin -dict cmudict-en-us.dict -hmm en-us-adapt -hyp adaptation-test.hyp
